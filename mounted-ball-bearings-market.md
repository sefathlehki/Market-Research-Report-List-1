<p><ul><li>Standard Duty</li><li>Washdown Duty</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mounted ball bearings are categorized into standard duty and washdown duty types, each serving specific operational needs. Standard duty bearings are designed for general applications, providing reliable performance under moderate loads and speeds. In contrast, washdown duty bearings are engineered to endure harsh environments, featuring materials and coatings that resist corrosion, moisture, and cleaning chemicals. These are ideal for applications in food processing, pharmaceuticals, and other sectors requiring stringent hygiene and durability measures.</p></p>
