<p><p>The hepatitis viruses diagnostic tests market includes various types of assays used to detect hepatitis infections. Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA) measures antibodies or antigens related to hepatitis. Recombinant Immunoblot Assay (RIBA) confirms positive EIA results through a more specific test. Transcription-Mediated Amplification (TMA) amplifies viral RNA for detection, especially for hepatitis C. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies DNA or RNA for precise virus identification. Other tests may include rapid test kits and serological assays, broadening the diagnostic landscape.</p></p>
