<p><p>Off-Circuit Tap-Changers (OCTCs) are essential devices used in electrical transformers to adjust the voltage levels by changing the transformer winding connections. Unlike on-load tap-changers, which operate while the transformer is energized, off-circuit tap-changers require the transformer to be de-energized for adjustments. This adds a layer of safety and precision in applications where load variations are predictable.</p><p>The Off-Circuit Tap-Changers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
