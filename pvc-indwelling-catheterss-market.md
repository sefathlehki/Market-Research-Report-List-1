<p><p>The PVC Indwelling Catheters market comprises various product types used for fluid drainage and management in medical settings. Indwelling urinary catheters facilitate urine drainage from the bladder, while indwelling plural catheters help remove excess fluid from the pleural space. Indwelling peritoneal catheters are used for dialysis, allowing fluid exchange in the peritoneal cavity, and indwelling nephrostomy catheters drain urine directly from the kidneys. Each type is designed for specific clinical applications, addressing diverse patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
