<p><p>The Retail Point-of-Sale (POS) terminals market is primarily divided into fixed and mobile POS terminals. Fixed POS terminals are stationary devices typically located at checkout counters, designed for high-volume transactions, featuring integrated hardware and software for processing sales, managing inventory, and handling payments securely. In contrast, mobile POS terminals offer portability, enabling transactions to occur anywhere within the retail space using smartphones or tablets. This flexibility enhances customer service and streamlines the checkout process, catering to modern retail needs.</p></p>
