<p><strong>The Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brazed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers</li><li>Gasketed Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers</li><li>Welded Plate & Frame Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plate and frame heat exchangers market comprises three main types: brazed, gasketed, and welded heat exchangers. Brazed plate heat exchangers feature plates joined by brazing, offering compact design and high thermal efficiency, ideal for HVAC and refrigeration. Gasketed plate heat exchangers use gaskets for sealing, allowing easy maintenance and cleaning, suitable for food processing and chemical industries. Welded plate heat exchangers, with plates welded together, provide durability and are used in high-pressure applications, often in oil and gas sectors.</p></p>
