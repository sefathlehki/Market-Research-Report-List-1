<p><p>The plastic granulators market features various cutting types, including double angle cut, chevron cut, and helical cut. Double angle cut granulators provide efficient and precise cutting by using blades positioned at dual angles, enhancing material processing. Chevron cut granulators utilize a zigzag blade pattern, improving shear and reducing plastic degradation. Helical cut granulators employ spiral blades for continuous cutting action, resulting in uniform granule size and reduced noise levels. These designs cater to distinct applications in recycling and plastic manufacturing processes.</p></p>
