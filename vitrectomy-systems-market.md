<p><p>The vitrectomy systems market is categorized by specific retinal conditions treated through surgical intervention. Retinal detachment involves reattaching the retina after separation, while macular pucker addresses distortion caused by scar tissue. Diabetic retinopathy focuses on managing diabetes-related eye damage, whereas macular holes involve repairing defects in the central retina. Vitreous hemorrhage addresses bleeding in the eye's gel-like substance, and vitreous floaters involve removing opacities that affect vision. The "others" category includes less common retinal conditions requiring similar surgical approaches.</p></p>
