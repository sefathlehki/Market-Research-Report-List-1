<p><p>The specialty alumina market includes various types that cater to specific industrial applications. Standard calcined alumina serves as a basic material for ceramics and refractories. Tabular alumina, with its high purity and toughness, is used in high-temperature applications. White fused alumina is known for its high abrasion resistance and is utilized in abrasive and refractory products. Medium and low soda calcined alumina types are designed for specific chemical properties, while "others" include niche variations catering to unique industry requirements.</p></p>
