<p><p>The Intelligent RTUs (Remote Terminal Units) market comprises two main types: Wireless RTUs and Wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs leverage technologies like cellular, satellite, and radio frequency for seamless data transmission without physical connections, offering flexibility and ease of installation. In contrast, Wired RTUs rely on traditional cabling systems, providing stable and secure communication in environments where signal interference may be a concern. Both types play a crucial role in remote monitoring and control applications across various industries, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
