<p><p>Ion sources are essential components in mass spectrometry and other analytical techniques, generating ions for measurement. The market is segmented into several types: </p><p>- **Electron Ionization (EI)** produces ions by bombarding gaseous samples with electrons, ideal for small molecules.</p><p>- **Chemical Ionization (CI)** employs reagent gases to ionize samples, offering softer ionization for larger molecules.</p><p>- **Gas Discharge Ion Sources** utilize electric fields to ionize gases, useful in various applications.</p><p>- **Others** include alternative methods like laser ablation and plasma ion sources, expanding the range of analytical capabilities.</p></p>
