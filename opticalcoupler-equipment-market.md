<p><p>The optical coupler equipment market is divided into two primary types: linear optocouplers and non-linear optocouplers. Linear optocouplers are designed for precise signal transmission and are often used in applications requiring linear amplification or analog signal processing. In contrast, non-linear optocouplers are suitable for digital applications, providing distinct ON/OFF states, making them ideal for switching and logic functions. These two categories cater to different electronic applications, highlighting the versatility and importance of optical coupling technology in various industries.</p></p>
