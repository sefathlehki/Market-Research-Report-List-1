<p><p>The laser skin resurfacing system market comprises two main types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layer of skin, effectively treating wrinkles, scars, and sun damage by promoting collagen production and rejuvenation. Non-ablative lasers, on the other hand, focus on the deeper layers of skin without damaging the surface, making them less invasive with minimal downtime. Both types cater to diverse skin concerns and patient preferences, contributing to their growing demand in cosmetic dermatology.</p></p>
