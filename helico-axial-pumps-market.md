<p><p>Helico-axial pumps are classified into two primary types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical helico-axial pumps are designed to operate in a vertical position, ideal for applications with limited floor space and often used in deep wells or underground applications. In contrast, horizontal helico-axial pumps are positioned horizontally, making them suitable for larger installations and easier maintenance. Both types are utilized in various industries, including water supply, irrigation, and industrial processes, offering efficient fluid movement with different spatial requirements.</p></p>
