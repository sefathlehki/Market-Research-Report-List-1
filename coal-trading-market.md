<p><p>Coal trading encompasses various types based on carbon content and energy yield. Lignite, the lowest rank, is mainly used for electricity generation due to its high moisture and low energy content. Sub-bituminous coal has higher energy output, serving both electricity and industrial applications. Bituminous coal, widely traded, is used for electricity and steel production, offering high energy content. Anthracite, the highest rank, features low impurities and is sought for heating and industrial processes due to its high carbon content and efficiency.</p></p>
