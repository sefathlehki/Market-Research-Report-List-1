<p><strong>The Surgical Table Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Surgical Table</li><li>Electrodynamic Surgical Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical table market comprises primarily two types: manual surgical tables and electrodynamic surgical tables. Manual surgical tables rely on mechanical components for adjustments, allowing operators to position patients through manual cranks and levers, making them cost-effective and straightforward. In contrast, electrodynamic surgical tables utilize electric motors for precise positioning and adjustments, enhancing efficiency and the surgeon's convenience during procedures. Both types cater to different operational needs and budgets within healthcare facilities, supporting a variety of surgical specialties.</p></p>
