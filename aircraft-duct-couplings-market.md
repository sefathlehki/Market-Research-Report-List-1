<p><p>The aircraft duct couplings market is divided into two main types: thread type and unthreaded type. Thread type couplings utilize threaded connections for secure assembly, ensuring tight seals and easy disassembly. These are often preferred for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, unthreaded type couplings rely on press-fit or clamp mechanisms, offering quick installations and reduced risk of cross-threading. Both types cater to specific aviation requirements, balancing performance, reliability, and maintenance considerations in various aircraft systems.</p></p>
