<p><strong>The Electronic Medical Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anal</li><li>Ear</li><li>Forehead</li><li>Oral</li><li>Axillary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic medical thermometer market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Anal thermometers provide accurate internal temperature readings, commonly used for infants. Ear thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure temperature quickly in the ear canal. Forehead thermometers, often non-contact, provide quick readings via infrared sensors, ideal for screening large groups. Oral thermometers are used under the tongue for accurate readings, while axillary thermometers measure temperature in the armpit, typically yielding lower accuracy but are easy to use.</p></p>
