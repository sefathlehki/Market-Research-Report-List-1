<p><p>The benchtop environmental chamber market encompasses three primary types: temperature chambers, humidity chambers, and combined temperature and humidity chambers. Temperature chambers are designed to simulate various thermal conditions, essential for testing material stability and product performance. Humidity chambers maintain specific moisture levels to evaluate the effects of humidity on different materials.
