<p><p>The home ventilator market comprises several types, primarily including Single-Level Ventilators, Bi-Level Ventilators, and Others. Single-Level Ventilators provide a consistent airflow at a set pressure, often used for patients with stable respiratory conditions. Bi-Level Ventilators offer two pressure settings for inspiration and expiration, catering to those with variable breathing needs. The "Others" category encompasses specialized devices like portable or adaptive ventilators tailored for specific patient requirements or conditions, enhancing personalized respiratory support in home care settings.</p></p>
