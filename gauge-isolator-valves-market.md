<p><p>The Gauge Isolator Valves market is segmented into two main types: single station and multi-station. Single station valves are designed for individual pressure gauge applications, providing isolation and protection for a single measurement point, ensuring accuracy and safety. In contrast, multi-station valves serve multiple gauges or instruments from a centralized point, allowing efficient management of several measurements simultaneously. This multi-station setup is ideal for complex systems needing robust monitoring while minimizing installation space and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
