<p><p>The Sialon powder market is categorized into three main types: α-Sialon, β-Sialon, and O-Sialon powders. α-Sialon is known for its high thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. β-Sialon exhibits excellent wear resistance and is often used in cutting tools. O-Sialon, distinguished by its oxygen content, combines properties of both α and β forms, offering enhanced performance in specific industrial applications. Together, these variants cater to diverse sectors such as ceramics, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
