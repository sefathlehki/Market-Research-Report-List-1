<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersion</li><li>Energy Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable X-ray Fluorescence (PXRF) Spectrometers market is categorized into two main types: wavelength dispersion and energy dispersion. Wavelength-dispersive PXRF spectrometers utilize a crystal to separate X-ray wavelengths, providing high resolution and sensitivity, ideal for trace element analysis. Conversely, energy-dispersive PXRF spectrometers detect X-ray energies directly, offering faster analysis and portability, making them suitable for on-site applications. Each type has unique advantages, catering to various applications in environmental testing, mining, and material characterization.</p></p>
