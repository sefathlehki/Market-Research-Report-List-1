<p><p>The ceramics material for additive manufacturing market is categorized into three main types: powder, resin, and others. </p><p>Powder ceramics consist of fine particles that are used in processes like selective laser sintering (SLS) and binder jetting, enabling the production of complex geometries. </p><p>Resin ceramics involve photopolymerization techniques, where light-sensitive liquids solidify into crystalline structures, suitable for detailed and intricate designs. </p><p>The "others" category encompasses alternative materials and methods, including composite materials and bio-ceramics, broadening application possibilities in various industries.</p></p>
