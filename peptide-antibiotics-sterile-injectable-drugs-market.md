<p><p>The peptide antibiotics sterile injectable drugs market can be categorized into ribosomal synthesized peptides and non-ribosomal synthesized peptides. Ribosomal synthesized peptides are produced through processes using ribosomes, translating mRNA into peptide chains, typically featuring natural structures and functions. Conversely, non-ribosomal synthesized peptides are generated by enzyme complexes, allowing for a diverse range of complex structures and modifications, often enhancing antimicrobial properties. Both types serve critical roles in addressing antibiotic resistance and providing effective therapeutic options in clinical settings.</p></p>
