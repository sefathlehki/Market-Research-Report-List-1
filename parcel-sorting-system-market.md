<p><p>The parcel sorting system market includes Horizontal Cross-band and Vertical Cross-band sorting machines, each designed to efficiently handle parcel logistics. Horizontal Cross-band Sorting Machines utilize a flat, horizontal conveyor system that allows parcels to be sorted laterally, providing quick and efficient sorting for larger volumes. In contrast, Vertical Cross-band Sorting Machines use an upward conveyor orientation to facilitate sorting in spaces with limited floor area, optimizing vertical storage. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and speed in parcel handling.</p></p>
