<p><p>The submarine seismograph market includes several types of devices designed for underwater seismic monitoring. Tethered systems are anchored to the seafloor, providing stable data collection. Self-floating seismographs drift with ocean currents but maintain stability for accurate readings. Cable seismographs are connected to the surface via cables, allowing real-time data transmission. Sputnik models are advanced systems that utilize buoyancy and satellite communication, enabling extensive coverage and remote monitoring of seismic activity, enhancing oceanographic research and disaster response capabilities.</p></p>
