<p><p>The residential stairlift market comprises primarily two types: straight and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for straightforward, linear staircases, featuring a simple track system that runs along the stairs. They are cost-effective and easier to install. In contrast, curved stairlifts are tailored for staircases with bends or multiple levels, requiring custom tracks. These offer greater flexibility and accessibility but come at a higher price due to their complexity. Both types enhance mobility and independence for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
