<p><p>The ballistic vests market comprises two main types: soft ballistic vests and hard ballistic vests. Soft ballistic vests are lightweight and designed for flexibility, providing protection against lower-caliber threats while ensuring comfort for extended wear. They are often used by law enforcement and security personnel. Hard ballistic vests, on the other hand, incorporate hard plates to offer enhanced protection against higher-caliber rounds and can withstand more severe ballistic threats. These vests are typically favored in military applications and high-risk scenarios.</p></p>
