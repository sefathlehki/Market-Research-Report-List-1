<p><strong>The Pump Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Positive-displacement</li><li>Diesel Engine</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pump controllers market is categorized into several types based on pump mechanisms. Centrifugal pump controllers manage pumps that use rotational energy to move fluids, commonly used in water and chemical applications. Positive-displacement controllers work with pumps that move fluid by trapping fixed amounts, making them ideal for precise dosing. Diesel engine controllers regulate pumps driven by diesel engines, often utilized in remote locations or for heavy-duty applications. Other categories may include specialty pumps like peristaltic and submersible types, catering to niche needs.</p></p>
