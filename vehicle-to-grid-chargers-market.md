<p><p>The Vehicle to Grid (V2G) chargers market is divided into two main types: AC V2G chargers and DC V2G chargers. AC V2G chargers utilize alternating current to facilitate bi-directional energy exchange between electric vehicles and the grid, primarily suited for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, DC V2G chargers employ direct current, allowing for faster charging and energy transfer, making them ideal for public charging stations and commercial fleets. Both types enhance grid stability and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
