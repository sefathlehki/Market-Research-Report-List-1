<p><p>The diaphragm pumps market is primarily categorized into air-operated and electrically operated types. Air-operated diaphragm pumps utilize compressed air to create suction, making them ideal for hazardous environments and where electricity poses a risk. They offer portability and simplicity in operation. Electrically operated diaphragm pumps, on the other hand, rely on electric motors for power, providing high efficiency and precise flow control. These pumps are suitable for various industrial applications, including chemical processing and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
