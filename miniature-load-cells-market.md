<p><ul><li>Reflection Type</li><li>Blocking Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature load cells market features two primary types: reflection type and blocking type. Reflection type load cells utilize a sensing element that reflects changes in force, providing high sensitivity and precision, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal space. Blocking type load cells, on the other hand, measure force by blocking a displacement, which is suited for heavier loads and industrial applications. Both types serve distinct purposes in various sectors, ensuring accurate weight measurement in compact designs.</p></p>
