<p><p>The Carcinoembryonic Antigen (CEA) Assay Kit market offers two primary testing methods: the Chemiluminescence Method and the Microparticle Enzyme-Linked Immunoassay (MEIA). The Chemiluminescence Method utilizes light-emitting reactions to quantify CEA levels, providing rapid and sensitive results. In contrast, MEIA employs microparticles coated with antibodies to detect CEA, combining high specificity and versatility. Both methods are integral for cancer diagnosis and monitoring, enabling healthcare providers to tailor treatment plans based on accurate biomarker levels.</p></p>
