<p><ul><li>Spindle Unit</li><li>ECU(Control Unit)</li><li>Touch Sensor(Pinch Sensor)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power liftgate systems market comprises three main types: Spindle Units, ECU (Control Unit), and Touch Sensors (Pinch Sensors). Spindle Units are mechanical components that facilitate the lifting and lowering of the liftgate. ECUs manage the overall operation, controlling the motor and sensor inputs for seamless functionality. Touch Sensors, also known as pinch sensors, detect obstacles, enhancing safety by preventing closure on objects or users. Together, these components contribute to the efficiency and convenience of modern liftgate systems in vehicles.</p></p>
