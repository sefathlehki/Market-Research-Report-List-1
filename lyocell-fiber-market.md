<p><p>The Lyocell fiber market comprises primarily two types: regular Lyocell fiber and crosslinked Lyocell fiber. Regular Lyocell fiber is known for its biodegradable properties and smooth texture, making it suitable for a variety of textiles, including apparel and home furnishings. Crosslinked Lyocell fiber, on the other hand, features enhanced stability and durability due to additional chemical crosslinking, making it ideal for applications demanding higher strength and resistance to environmental factors. Both types contribute significantly to sustainable textile solutions.</p></p>
