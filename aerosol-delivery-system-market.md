<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aerosol Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Small-volume Nebulizer (SVN)</li><li>Pressurized Metered-dose Inhaler (pMDI)</li><li>Dry Powder Inhaler (DPI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol delivery system market comprises three primary types: Small-volume nebulizers (SVN), which convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation; pressurized metered-dose inhalers (pMDI), which release a specific dose of medication in aerosol form using a propellant; and dry powder inhalers (DPI), which deliver medication as a dry powder that patients inhale. Each device serves different patient needs and preferences, optimizing the delivery of respiratory medications for conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>
