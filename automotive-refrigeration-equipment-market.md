<p><ul><li>Single Temperature Type</li><li>Multi-Temperature Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive refrigeration equipment market is categorized into two main types: single temperature type and multi-temperature type. Single temperature type systems maintain a consistent temperature, ideal for transporting goods that require uniform cooling, such as pharmaceuticals or perishables. In contrast, multi-temperature type systems offer the flexibility to compartmentalize cargo, allowing different temperature zones within the same unit. This versatility is essential for transporting various products simultaneously, enabling better management of diverse loads and enhancing operational efficiency in logistics.</p></p>
