<p><p>The Micro Absorbable Suture Anchor market is classified into two primary insertion types: "Thread-In Insertion" and "Push-In Insertion." Thread-In Insertion involves a suture anchor that is threaded into the bone, allowing for secure attachment of soft tissue. This method provides strong mechanical stability. Conversely, Push-In Insertion allows the anchor to be pushed directly into the bone without threading, facilitating a less invasive procedure and quicker deployment. Both methods are designed to enhance surgical outcomes in soft tissue repairs.</p></p>
