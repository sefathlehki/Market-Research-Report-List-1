<p><strong>The RF Power Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Type Power Meter</li><li>Through Type Power Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF power meter market is primarily divided into two types: absorption type and through type power meters. Absorption type power meters measure the power by absorbing the RF signal and converting it to heat, offering accurate readings for low to moderate power levels. In contrast, through type power meters allow the RF signal to pass through the device while utilizing a secondary sensing element to gauge the power level, making them suitable for higher power applications and continuous monitoring.</p></p>
