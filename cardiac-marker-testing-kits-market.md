Other markers may include BNP and hs-CRP, offering insights into heart failure and inflammation. These tests enable prompt decision-making in clinical settings, enhancing the management of cardiovascular disease and improving patient outcomes. The market for these kits is driven by increasing cardiovascular diseases globally.</p></p>
