<p><strong>The VOC Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) monitoring equipment market primarily includes two key types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gases, providing sensitive and real-time detection of a wide range of VOCs. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance to detect VOCs, offering cost-effective solutions for various applications. Both types play essential roles in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and indoor air quality assessment.</p></p>
