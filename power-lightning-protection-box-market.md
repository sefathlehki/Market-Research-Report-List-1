<p><p>The Power Lightning Protection Box market is categorized into two primary types: Tandem Type and Parallel Type. </p><p>Tandem Type systems protect against surges by connecting two or more devices in a series configuration, enhancing protection levels through redundancy. In contrast, Parallel Type systems distribute surge protection across multiple parallel devices, allowing for simultaneous operation and improved reliability. Both types aim to safeguard electrical systems from lightning strikes and surges, but they differ in their design and effectiveness in specific applications.</p></p>
