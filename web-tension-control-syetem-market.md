<p><p>The Web Tension Control System market is segmented into Open Loop and Closed Loop systems. Open Loop systems operate without feedback, relying on preset parameters to manage tension, making them simpler but less precise. In contrast, Closed Loop systems utilize real-time feedback from sensors to continuously adjust tension, ensuring tighter control and improved accuracy. These distinctions affect various industries, such as packaging and printing, where maintaining optimal web tension is crucial for product quality and operational efficiency.</p></p>
