<p><p>The solar-powered UAV market comprises three main types: fixed-wing UAVs, rotorcraft UAVs, and umbrella UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs are designed for long-range missions with high efficiency aerodynamic designs, ideal for surveillance and mapping. Rotorcraft UAVs, including quadcopters, offer vertical take-off and landing capabilities, suited for tasks requiring maneuverability in confined spaces. Umbrella UAVs feature unique designs for specific applications, combining elements of both fixed-wing and rotorcraft, enhancing versatility in various operational environments, particularly in remote areas where battery limitations can be a challenge.</p></p>
