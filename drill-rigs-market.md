<p><p>The drill rigs market is primarily categorized into pneumatic and hydraulic drill rigs. Pneumatic drill rigs utilize compressed air for drilling operations, making them suitable for various terrains and providing mobility and efficiency. In contrast, hydraulic drill rigs employ pressurized fluids to generate power, offering superior torque and the ability to perform heavy-duty tasks. Each type serves distinct applications in sectors such as construction, mining, and oil exploration, reflecting their specialized capabilities and operational efficiency.</p></p>
