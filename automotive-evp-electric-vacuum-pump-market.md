<p><p>The Automotive Electric Vacuum Pump (EVP) market comprises three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. Diaphragm type pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create vacuum pressure, offering compact design and reliability. Leaf type pumps utilize oscillating blades for efficient airflow, typically being lightweight and compact. Swing piston type pumps feature a rotating piston mechanism, providing high efficiency and robust performance. Each type serves specific vehicle applications, adapting to varying power requirements and space constraints.</p></p>
