Fully automatic tapping machines operate independently, requiring minimal human intervention, and are designed for high efficiency and precision in mass production settings. In contrast, semi-automatic tapping machines require some manual input for operation, providing flexibility and lower capital investment, making them suitable for smaller-scale manufacturing or diverse production runs. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, enhancing productivity in metalworking processes.</p></p>
