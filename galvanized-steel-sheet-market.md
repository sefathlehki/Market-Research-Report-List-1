<p><strong>The Galvanized Steel Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Dip Galvanized Steel Sheet</li><li>Electro Galvanized Steel Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized steel sheet market consists primarily of two types: hot dip galvanized (HDG) and electro galvanized sheets. Hot dip galvanized sheets are produced by immersing steel in molten zinc, providing robust corrosion resistance and durability, making them ideal for construction and automotive applications. In contrast, electro galvanized sheets are coated with zinc through an electroplating process, offering a thinner layer of protection. This method suits applications requiring good surface finish and precision, like appliances and electronics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
