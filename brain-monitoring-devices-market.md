<p><p>The brain monitoring devices market encompasses various technologies used to assess and monitor brain activity and health. EEG devices capture electrical activity through electrodes, providing insights into neurological functions. MEG devices measure magnetic fields generated by neuronal activity, offering high spatial and temporal resolution. TCD devices assess cerebral blood flow via ultrasound, essential for stroke monitoring. ICP monitors gauge intracranial pressure, critical for traumatic brain injury management. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen saturation in the brain, aiding in ischemic injury evaluation.</p></p>
