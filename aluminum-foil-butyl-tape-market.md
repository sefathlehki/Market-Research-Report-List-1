<p><p>Aluminum foil butyl tape is available in two primary types: single-sided and double-faced. Single-sided tape features a sticky butyl adhesive on one side, making it ideal for applications where bonding or sealing is needed on a flat surface. In contrast, double-faced tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing it to bond two surfaces together effectively. Both types are commonly used in construction, automotive, and HVAC industries for weatherproofing, insulation, and sealing joints, providing durability and moisture resistance.</p></p>
