<p><p>The tubular burner market is primarily divided into two types: steel type and metal fiber type. Steel type burners are known for their durability and ability to withstand high temperatures, making them suitable for heavy industrial applications. In contrast, metal fiber type burners utilize a lightweight, porous metal fiber structure, offering improved combustion efficiency and lower emissions. Both types cater to various industries, including food processing, ceramics, and heating, with applications benefitting from their unique properties in performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
