<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace tire market consists primarily of two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature plies that are arranged at a 90-degree angle to the tread, allowing for better fuel efficiency, enhanced performance, and improved wear resistance. In contrast, bias tires have plies that crisscross at various angles, providing greater strength and durability but typically less performance efficiency. Each tire type serves specific aircraft needs, influencing factors such as landing gear design and operational conditions.</p></p>
