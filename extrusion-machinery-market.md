<p><strong>The Extrusion Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Extrusion</li><li>Twin Screw Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extrusion machinery market is primarily segmented into two types: single screw extrusion and twin screw extrusion. Single screw extrusion involves a single rotating screw that conveys and processes materials, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for a variety of applications. In contrast, twin screw extrusion utilizes two intermeshing screws that enhance mixing, compounding, and uniformity, making it ideal for complex materials and high processing speeds. Each type caters to different production needs and material characteristics in the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
