<p><strong>The Polymer Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Polymer Blend</li><li>Liquid Polymer Blend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymer Injection System market is primarily segmented into two types: Dry Polymer Blend and Liquid Polymer Blend. </p><p>Dry Polymer Blend involves powdered polymers that are typically mixed with water at the injection site, allowing for precise control over the concentration and viscosity. This method enhances shelf life and reduces transportation costs. </p><p>In contrast, Liquid Polymer Blend consists of pre-dissolved polymers delivered directly to the application site, offering immediate usability but potentially higher transport costs. Both types cater to various applications in water treatment, mining, and construction.</p></p>
