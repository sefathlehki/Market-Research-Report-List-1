<p><p>The fully automatic thinning machine market is segmented into single-axis and biaxial types. In contrast, biaxial machines work across two axes, providing enhanced maneuverability and efficiency, making them ideal for complex terrains and diverse cropping systems. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, with single-axis machines focusing on cost-effectiveness, while biaxial options offer versatility for larger and more intricate farming operations.</p></p>
