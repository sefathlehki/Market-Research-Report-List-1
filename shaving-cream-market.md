<p><p>The shaving cream market can be categorized primarily into two segments: aftershave and pre-shave products. On the other hand, pre-shave products are used before the shaving process to prepare the skin and hair, enhancing the shaving experience by softening whiskers and creating a protective barrier. Both types cater to specific grooming needs, emphasizing skin health and comfort.</p></p>
